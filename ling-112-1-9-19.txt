The child buried the statue with a shovel.
R = reading
R1: The child used a shovel to bury the statue.
    ("used" shows that the shovel was used to bury)
R2: The child buried the statue and the shovel together.
    ("together" shows that they are a unit)
R3: The child buried the statue that had a shovel attached to it.
    (similar but the statue and shovel are connected because of "attached")

"the the child" is a grammatical sentences
